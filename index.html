Here's a timeline of Alexander Hamilton's life:

  • 1755 - Alexander Hamilton was born in Charlestown, Nevis.
  • 1777 - Hamilton became General George Washington's assistant.
  • 1780 - Hamilton married Elizabeth Schuyler.
  • 1787 - Hamilton was a New York delegate at the Constitutional Convention.
  • 1804 - Hamilton was shot by Aaron Burr and died.
